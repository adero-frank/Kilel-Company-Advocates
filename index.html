Our Legacy of Excellence

A Tradition of Legal Excellence

Founded in 1985, Chawla & Co Advocates is a trusted law firm partnership in the region. Our founding partners envisioned a practice that would not only provide exceptional legal counsel but would also serve as a pillar of integrity in the legal community. Specializing in Banking & Securities Law, we serve clients across Kenya with a team of 11-50 dedicated professionals.

Over the past 40 years, we have built a strong foundation of success by approaching each case with meticulous attention to detail, strategic foresight, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' objectives.
